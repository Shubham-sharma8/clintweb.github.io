Cookie Editor - For Safari 

A cookie editor for Safari is a tool or software that allows you to view, edit, and manage cookies stored by the Safari web browser. Cookies are small text files that websites store on your computer to store information about your browsing habits, preferences, and other data.

  • Cookie Viewing: It enables you to see the cookies stored by Safari, including details like cookie name, value, expiration date, and domain.
  • Cookie Editing: You can modify the values of existing cookies or create new cookies manually. This can be useful for testing or troubleshooting purposes.
  • Cookie Management: You can delete unwanted cookies, sort cookies by domain or expiration date, and organize them for better management.

Ad Blocker - For Safari

An ad blocker for Safari is a software or browser extension that helps block unwanted advertisements and other types of online advertising content while you browse the internet using the Safari web browser. Ad blockers work by detecting and filtering out ads from websites, preventing them from being displayed on your screen.

Ad Blocking: Ad blockers automatically identify and block various forms of advertisements, such as banner ads, pop-ups, video ads, interstitials, and more. By doing so, they enhance your browsing experience by reducing distractions and minimizing the intrusion of ads.

Customizable Filters: Many ad blockers allow users to customize their ad-blocking experience. You can create whitelists or blacklists to permit or block ads on specific websites. This flexibility enables you to tailor your ad-blocking preferences according to your needs.
