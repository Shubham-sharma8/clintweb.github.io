FEATURES

The cookie Editor - For Safari offers a variety of powerful features to enhance your control over cookies and browsing privacy

Cookie Viewing

Easily view and analyse the cookies stored by your web browser Get detailed information such as cookie names, values, expiration dates, and domains.

Cookie Editing

Edit and modify the values of existing cookies to customize your browsing experience. This feature enables you to adjust cookie settings according to your preferences.

Cookie Creation

Create new cookies manually to test specific scenarios or simulate different browsing conditions. This allows you to have more control over the cookies stored by websites.

Cookie Management

Organize and manage cookies effectively. Sort and filter cookies based on various criteria such as domain, expiration date, or name. Delete unwanted cookies effortlessly.

Import and Export

Import cookies from external sources or export cookies for backup or sharing purposes. This feature enables seamless transfer of cookies between devices

User-Friendly Interface

Enjoy a user-friendly and intuitive interface that makes cookie management and editing hassle-free. The cookie editor is designed to be accessible even for non-technical users.
